Egg Drop Soup Recipe

Description

This is a classic recipe for a delicious staple in most Eastern societies.

It has become a global dish thanks to modern immigration.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Broth
  • Large Egg (2)
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Heat the Chick Broth to a boil
  2. Reduce heat to simmer
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl
  4. Drizzle the egg into the Chicken Broth while stirring
  5. Reduce to low heat
  6. Chop Green Onions into small pieces
  7. Garnish Soup with chopped onions
