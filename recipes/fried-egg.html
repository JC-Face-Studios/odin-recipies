Fried Egg Recipe

Description

This is one of the best recipes to have.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Oil or Butter
  • Eggs (2)

Steps

  1. Preheat the pan to low-medium heat
  2. Add two (2) tablespoons of oil/butter to the pan
  3. Crack each egg into the pan
  4. Flip each egg once the whites are mostly cooked
