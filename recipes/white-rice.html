White Rice Recipe

Description

Everyone loves this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Long-grain White Rice (1 cup)
  • Water (1.5 cups)

Steps

  1. Add rice and water to pot
  2. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to simmer
  3. Cover the pot with a lid
  4. Cook for 15 minutes without removing the lid
  5. Remove from heat and let sit for 10 minutes
  6. Fluff with fork and serve
